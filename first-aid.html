First-Aid Basics

Essential steps for common injuries. This is not a substitute for professional medical help.

Cuts & Scrapes

  1. Stop the Bleeding: Apply gentle, direct pressure with a clean cloth.
  2. Clean the Wound: Rinse with clean water. Keep soap out of the wound.
  3. Apply Antibiotic: Use a thin layer of antibiotic ointment.
  4. Cover the Wound: Apply a sterile bandage.

Minor Burns

  1. Cool the Burn: Hold under cool (not cold) running water for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Remove Jewelry: Gently remove rings or other tight items from the burned area.
  3. Don't Break Blisters: Leave blisters intact to prevent infection.
  4. Cover Loosely: Apply a sterile gauze bandage.

Sprains (Ankle, Wrist)

Remember R.I.C.E.:

  • Rest: Avoid activities that cause pain.
  • Ice: Apply an ice pack for 15-20 minutes every 2-3 hours.
  • Compression: Wear an elastic compression bandage.
  • Elevation: Raise the injured limb above your heart level.
